How long does delivery take?

+

We deliver your products within 2-3 business days from the date of order confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Which areas do you deliver to?

+

We offer PAN India shipping! We deliver to every corner of India, from metro cities to remote villages across all states and union territories.

Which courier services do you use?

+

We ship via Standard Delivery service and India Post Speedpost to ensure your orders reach you safely and on time. You'll receive tracking details once your order is dispatched.

Can I track my order?

+

Yes! You can track your order in real-time using our tracking system. Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS.

What if I'm not available during delivery?

+

Our delivery partners will attempt delivery 2-3 times. If you're unavailable, they'll leave a delivery notice. You can also contact our support team to reschedule the delivery at your convenience.

Are there any shipping charges?

+

Shipping charges may vary based on your location and order value. Many orders qualify for free shipping. You'll see the exact shipping cost at checkout before placing your order.

How is my order packaged?

+

All products are carefully packed and quality-checked before dispatch. We use secure packaging materials to ensure your items reach you in perfect condition.

üîÑ Return & Refund Questions

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 3-day return window. You can return products within 3 days after delivery for a full refund or exchange, provided the item meets our return conditions.

How do I initiate a return?

+

Contact our customer support team via phone, email, or through our website within 3 days of delivery. Provide your order details and reason for return. We'll guide you through the process and provide a return authorization.

What items can be returned?

+

You can return products that are damaged, defective, wrong items delivered, don't match description, or if you've changed your mind (for unused items). The product must be in its original condition with all tags and packaging intact.

What items cannot be returned?

+

Non-returnable items include perishable goods, personal care and hygiene products, intimate items, clearance sale items (unless defective), customized products, and digital products.

How long does the refund take?

+

Once we receive and verify your returned item, refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

Do I need to pay for return shipping?

+

For damaged or defective products, we provide a free return shipping label. For other returns, return shipping charges may apply based on the reason for return. Our support team will clarify this when you initiate the return.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

+

Yes! You can request an exchange for a different size, color, or variant within the 3-day return window. Contact our support team to arrange an exchange, subject to product availability.

What conditions must be met for a successful return?

+

Items must be returned within 3 days, in unused and unwashed condition, with original packaging, tags, and labels intact. All accessories and free gifts must be included. The product should not be damaged due to misuse.
