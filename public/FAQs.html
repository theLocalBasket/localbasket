Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?
+
Delivery usually takes 2–3 business days from order confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.
Do you deliver across India?
+
Yes! We offer PAN-India delivery — reaching metros, towns, and remote areas across all states and union territories.
Can I track my order?
+
Absolutely. Once shipped, you’ll receive an SMS and email with a tracking link to check your order status anytime.

Payments & Orders

What payment options are available?
+
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and wallets. All payments are securely processed through encrypted gateways.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
+
Yes, you can modify or cancel within 1 hour of placing your order. Please contact support immediately for help.
Are all products authentic and handmade?
+
100%! Every product is sourced from verified local artisans and small-scale creators — ensuring authenticity and quality.
